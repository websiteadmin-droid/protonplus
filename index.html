PROTONPLUS

Proton Plus incorporated in 2023 to cater to the E-Mobility segment with complete manufacturing solutions. Our expertise spans design, simulation, development, prototyping, validation, pre-production, and low volume production.

With close collaboration with our customers, we manage complete vehicle & vehicle parts development from ideation to SOP. Our core values of Problem Solving, Passion, and Professionalism drive everything we do.

Our Expertise

End-to-end capabilities across design, prototyping, tooling and low-volume production — focused on E‑Mobility components.

Exterior Automotive Parts

Design and manufacture of exterior components including bumpers, body panels, grilles, mirrors, fascias, and aerodynamic trim. We provide prototyping, tooling and low-volume production with surface-finish attention for paint-ready parts.

Interior Automotive Parts

Interior systems and components such as instrument panels, console housings, door trims, air vents, bezels and brackets — delivered as prototypes, validated parts and low-volume production ready items with functional and aesthetic quality.
